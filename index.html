our services

System Description

We provide an interactive IDX landing page designed specifically to convert clicks into leads. The site integrates with Google Ads, Bing and the CRM you use. We effectively generate leads by leveraging technical performance and making the most out of your advertising budget. No need to adopt any new system, we will integrate with how you operate so you can keep all your processes in place. This system is very low touch for our clients.

Pricing

The system is an out of the box solution. One time set up fee of $300 and a monthly service fee of $350. The set up fee includes gaining access to your MLS data feed, setting up the site with a domain, setting up advertisments and integrating with your CRM. This is in addition to your ppc budget which will be passed directly to Google or Bing. Any budget over $1000 per month will be charged an additional $75 managment fee. Clients will also be responsable for MLS board fees, if needed(most are free).

Service

We offer territiorial exclusivity with two total licenses per terratory. One license is available for Google and one for Bing. As long as you are a client we will not provide our service to agents in your market on the same advertising platform. If you choose to use both Google and Bing there will be a discount extended for claiming both licenses. Our service is offered on a month to month basis.
